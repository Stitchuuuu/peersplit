PeerSplit is a free app that helps you split and track group expenses with ease.

Its peer-to-peer, so your data stays completely privatenothing is stored on the cloud.

Got feedback or ideas? Reach out to me @tanayvk on X, or create an issue on GitHub.

FEATURES

- 100% freeno signup required

- Local-first and works fully offline

- Cross-platform: use it on mobile, desktop, or laptop

- Peer-to-peer: collaborate with friends while keeping your data private

- Smooth UX that stays out of your way

- Available in dark and light modes

- Import from Splitwise, export to CSV

QUICK START GUIDE

PeerSplit works directly in your browser, so you can use it as a web app instantly or install it as a PWA (Progressive Web App) for an even better experience. Heres how:

  1. Open Chrome and visit the PeerSplit website.
  2. Tap the menu icon (three dots) in the top-right corner.
  3. Select "Add to Home screen".
  4. Follow the prompts to install the app and create a shortcut on your home screen.
  5. Now, you can launch PeerSplit like any other app from your home screen!
  1. Open Safari or Chrome and navigate to the PeerSplit website.
  2. Tap the Share icon at the bottom (the square with an arrow pointing up).
  3. Scroll down and select "Add to Home Screen".
  4. Choose a name for the app (PeerSplit is default) and tap "Add".
  5. PeerSplit will now appear on your home screen, ready to use like a regular app!
  1. Open Chrome or Microsoft Edge and visit the PeerSplit website.
  2. Look for the install icon (a small plus sign in the address bar) and click it.
  3. Follow the prompts to install PeerSplit as a desktop app.
  4. Once installed, you can open PeerSplit from your Start menu or desktop.
